About the Event

Unleash your potential, innovate with purpose, and build the future of technology.

Join us at **Hackverse-25**, the premier national-level hackathon hosted by **CMR College of Engineering & Technology**. This 24-hour event is a platform for innovative minds to explore and create solutions in a diverse range of technological fields. We invite students from across the country to collaborate, learn from industry experts, and turn their groundbreaking ideas into reality.

Hackverse is your chance to challenge the status quo, hone your technical skills, and make a tangible impact through technology. It's more than just a competition; it's a launchpad for future leaders and a celebration of creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving.

Where: CMR College of Engineering & Technology, Kandlakoya, Hyderabad

When: Wednesday, September 24, 2025 – Thursday, September 25, 2025

Main Themes of Hackverse

Explore a diverse range of themes designed to challenge your creativity and technical prowess.

AI & Machine Learning

Develop intelligent systems using neural networks, data science, and advanced algorithms.

Cybersecurity

Build robust security solutions and ethical hacking tools for a safer digital world.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Create smart, connected devices for applications in home automation and industrial systems.

Blockchain Technology

Work with distributed ledger technology to build secure and decentralized applications.

Quantum Computing

Delve into the future of computing with projects focused on quantum algorithms and cryptography.

Sustainable Technology

Develop innovative solutions for a greener future, addressing environmental challenges with technology.
