About the Event

Join us at Hackverse-25, the premier national-level hackathon hosted by CMR College of Engineering & Technology. This 24-hour event is a platform for innovative minds to explore and create solutions in a diverse range of technological fields. We invite students from across the country to collaborate, learn from industry experts, and turn their groundbreaking ideas into reality. Hackverse is your chance to challenge the status quo, hone your technical skills, and make a tangible impact through technology.

Where

CMR College of Engineering & Technology, Kandlakoya, Hyderabad

When

Wednesday, September 24, 2025 – Thursday, September 25, 2025

Main Themes of Hackverse

Explore a diverse range of themes designed to challenge your creativity and technical prowess.

AI & Machine Learning

Develop intelligent systems using neural networks, data science, and advanced algorithms.

Cybersecurity

Build robust security solutions and ethical hacking tools for a safer digital world.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Create smart, connected devices for applications in home automation and industrial systems.

Blockchain Technology

Work with distributed ledger technology to build secure and decentralized applications.

Quantum Computing

Delve into the future of computing with projects focused on quantum algorithms and cryptography.

Sustainable Technology

Develop innovative solutions for a greener future, addressing environmental challenges with technology.

Hackverse-25 GUIDELINES

Team Formation

Each team should consist of 2-4 members with diverse skill sets.

Registration Fee

A non-refundable fee of Rs. 1500 per team is required for registration.

Eligibility

Open to all college students. A valid student ID is mandatory for verification.

Project Development

All work must be done during the hackathon. No pre-existing projects.

Equipment

Participants must bring their own laptops and chargers. Internet will be provided.

Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated on creativity, technical difficulty, impact, and presentation.
